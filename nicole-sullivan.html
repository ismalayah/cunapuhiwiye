Things You Didn't Know About Nicole Sullivan

July 30, 2024

MADtv series actress, Nicole Sullivan, is best known for her portrayal of characters in the movies Heaven's Floor, Surprise Me!, All-Stars, Eat with Me, Jackhammer, and 17 Again. 

However, Sullivan is famous for her TV series rather than her movies. Her methodological acting mused all the viewers back in six series of MADtv and five seasons of The King of Queens.

Family Details of Sullivan

Family Guy's starlet was born on 21st April 1970 in Manhattan, New York to her father Edward C. Sullivan and her mother Madonna Sullivan.

More, she has a sibling Jennifer Sullivan. Nicole was raised in New York along with her sister.

Networth of Nicole Sullivan

Sullivan is a rich actress with a total net worth of $7 million. Her wealth includes her salary, a mansion in Los Angeles worth $1.8 million, her cars, and more.

 Therefore, the TV personality is living a lavish life with her family in California.

Is Nicole Married?

Inside Job's actress is married to an American actor Jason Packham on 14th May 2006. Packham played along with his wife in The King of Queens in 1998.

While acting in the same TV series, the love pair fell in love and started dating. More, Sullivan is happily cherishing her 15-years long married life.

Dating History of Sullivan

The All Rise starlet was in a relationship with Seth MacFarlane in her early days. They came across in 1998 while working together in Family Guy.

However, MacFarlane broke up with his sweetheart in the year 2000. Later, Sullivan found her soulmate and married Packham.

Who Are Sullivan's Children?

The actor couple shares two sons. in May 2007, their first son Dashel Pierce Packham was born.

Soon in August 2009, Dashel was followed by his young brother, Beckett Edward Packham.

Education of Sullivan

HouseBroken's actress is a well-educated lady. She completed her high school at Middleburgh High School in 1987. Later, she attended the British American Drama Academy and Northwestern University.

Sullivan also went to the Northwestern University School of Communication. Hence, she is not only an acting fanatic but also a communication student. 

Sullivan's Health Issues

In 2010, Sullivan caught H1N1 which led her to ICU for 18 days. According to her Instagram post on 2nd April 2020, Sullivan reports that she had 105.8 degrees fever, dry cough, and double bi-lateral sepsis pneumonia.

The antibiotic medications and proper health care cured her back to a healthy life once again.

Pet Lover Nicole Sullivan

The DIVOS! actress loves dogs and has a pet of Pit Bull breed. Sullivan shares pretty many photos of her children playing with their dogs.

More, Sullivan also loves other animals including cats. Her Instagram profile is full of animal-friendly pictures.
