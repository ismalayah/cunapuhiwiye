The 10 strongest Hells Paradise characters

August 01, 2024
blog

Those who enjoyed Yuji Kaku’s manga Hell’s Paradise have patiently waited for the television series Hell’s Paradise: Jigokuraku to air. And now, just a few episodes in, the show has found tremendous popularity by combining elements of historical fiction with those of action, adventure, and horror. The story’s heartbeat is unquestionably its eclectic array of characters, each exhibiting unique strengths that set them apart.

The main character is Gabimaru the Hollow, a ninja from Iwagakure Village famous for his extraordinary fighting skills and cold demeanor. The story follows the inmates on death row and Yamada Asaemon executioners, including the upstanding Sagiri, as they accompany Gabimaru to the island where the legendary elixir of life is said to be. Although it has the reputation of being a “paradise,” this island is far from ideal. 

As the story progresses, each character must deal with their own demons and ethical dilemmas. In the face of overwhelming obstacles, these strong characters exhibit various strengths, including physical prowess, strategic intelligence, emotional resilience, and moral courage.

10. Nurugai

Nurugai brings a refreshingly unusual mix of naiveté, bravery, and maturing strength to the story. Raised as a warrior from birth, Nurugai is a member of the Yamada Asaemon tribe. She might look young and frail, but she has much fighting experience. While her prowess in battle is undeniable, it is in her relationships with others that Nurugai truly shines. Her genuine compassion injects some humanity into the island’s otherwise harsh setting. 

Despite the unforgiving nature of her environment, Nurugai never loses her sense of empathy or concern for her fellow soldiers. Her growth as a character is based on her internal battle to reconcile her youthful naivety with the harsh truths of the world. Nurugai undergoes substantial development, coming into her own as a warrior and a caring human being as she confronts the island’s brutalities and wrestles with her friends’ complexities.

9. Aza Tōma

Tōma, the younger twin brother of Aza Chōbē, makes his way through the dangerous island while trying to find his own identity and strength. At first, Tōma may seem like the more vulnerable of the Aza brothers, but he is rather powerful. Although his fighting style isn’t as dominant as Chōbē’s, Tōma is a capable fighter in his own right. His strength is not in physical prowess but in his quick wit and ability to adjust to new situations.

Tōma loves that his brother looks out for him, but he also wants to go out independently and establish himself. His growth as a character is heavily dependent on this underlying dynamic conflict. Through the trials of island life, Tōma matures and becomes more fully himself throughout the show. His strong loyalty to his sibling and ambition to forge his own path gives him a fascinating allure.

8. Shija

Despite not having any special powers, the warrior monk Shija stands out due to his extraordinary physical prowess, skill in battle, and unyielding will. Shija, a government-approved executioner known as an Asaemon, stands out because of his keen intellect. Due to his sharp eye for detail and levelheaded analysis, he is an excellent contributor to the group. The gang relies heavily on his analytical approach to the island’s perils, which often yields tactical advantages.

But Shija’s mind is capable of much more than just strategizing. Because of his heritage as an Asaemon, he is a formidable swordsman who can protect himself from the island’s lethal natives. His fighting technique is a reflection of his brilliant mind, with an emphasis on well-placed blows and delicate footwork. Even in his dealings with other characters, Shija’s logical mindset shines through. The detached, observant nature of his relationships helps distinguish him as the team’s strategic thinker.

7. Yuzuriha

Yuzuriha is a memorable character because she exemplifies a rare combination of swiftness, intelligence, and tenacity. As the group’s kunoichi (female ninja), she shows to be an invaluable asset as they traverse the treacherous island. Yuzuriha’s fighting style is unusual from her contemporaries since she emphasizes strategy, agility, and deceit above raw strength. She has the upper hand even when up against seemingly insurmountable odds, thanks to her skill at creating illusions and manipulating her adversaries. 

Yuzuriha’s practical outlook on life is one of her most distinguishing traits. Yuzuriha’s primary goal for her journey is to ensure her own survival, in contrast to the more complex reasons other characters give. Even though her practicality often puts her at odds with the rest of the gang, it ultimately adds a new dimension to their dynamic and helps them see their situation differently. Yuzuriha comes across as self-centered at the beginning of the series, but she develops loyalty and selflessness as the story progresses.

6. Sagiri of the Yamada Asaemon

Sagiri of the Yamada Asaemon, the only woman among the Asaemon, is a superb swordswoman who can hold her own against the dangerous creatures that inhabit the island. Her fighting style is characterized by speed, agility, and the ability to surprise her opponents with savagery. Sagiri is also an exceptional team member because she possesses qualities beyond her physical strength, such as sensitivity and compassion. She is the group’s conscience, keeping them grounded in humanity despite the grim reality of their mission. Her kindness and sense of fairness often place her at odds with the callousness of their mission. 

Personal development is also a hallmark of Sagiri’s time on the island. Being the sole woman in her field initially made Sagiri doubt her abilities, but she eventually learned to embrace her uniqueness and stand in her own power. One fascinating feature of her personality is how she changed from a hesitant executioner to a courageous and caring warrior. Sagiri’s capacity to overcome bias and see things from other people’s perspectives is on full display as, despite her initial mistrust of Gabimaru, she and he grow close over time.

5. Tamiya Gantetsusai

Swordsman Tamiya Gantetsusai is as unpredictable as he is powerful due to his fiery temperament and insatiable desire for combat. Gantetsusai, born into the samurai class, sold his honor for a life of crime. Gantetsusai rushes at the chance to win his freedom by embarking on a risky trip to the unknown island in pursuit of the elixir of life, seeing it as an opportunity for liberty and intense fighting. 

Gantetsusai’s willingness to risk death for a cause reflects who he is. He is fearless in the face of danger, charging headfirst into battle against foes that may be out of his league. His carefree attitude can sometimes endanger him and his friends, straining his ties with them. Gantetsusai may come across as gruff, but he reveals his softer side when interacting with Fuchi, a fellow convict and his mission companion, revealing an unexpected bond that enriches his personality.

4. Rien

One of the main adversaries, Rien, is an intriguing and terrifying figure. Rien, a Tensen, personifies the enormous obstacles our heroes face as they attempt to make their way across the dangerous island they call home. 

Rien is portrayed as a fearsome foe due to his extraordinary Tao abilities and comprehensive knowledge of the Elixir of Life. With his threatening intellect and drive for immortality, he is easily one of the most formidable characters in the series. Because of his callous disregard for human life and his unrelenting pursuit of his own interests, he frequently finds himself at odds with the heroes, particularly Gabimaru.

3. Aza Chōbē

A seasoned criminal with a fearsome reputation, Aza Chōbē is a feared warrior due to his exceptional physical prowess. His approach to combat is ruthless and straightforward, favoring power over finesse. He is a crucial member of the death row inmates searching for the elixir of life thanks to his unrelenting determination. Yet, Chōbē is not a simple brute. His bond with his twin brother, Tōma, deeply shapes his character. 

Chōbē’s caring for Tōma starkly contrasts his ruthlessness, showing a softer side to his personality. Chōbē’s protective instincts are integral to his character journey but can quickly morph into a possessive and dominating attitude towards Tōma. The conflict between his protective instincts and the need to respect Tōma’s autonomy is a fascinating dynamic that adds richness to the story.

2. Jikka of the Yamada Asaemon

Jikka is the unifying force of the Asaemon as their leader while they venture through the dangerous island. Jikka’s extraordinary skill in battle is one of his most distinguishing features. Because of his Asaemon background, he is a skilled swordsman who can defend himself against the island’s deadly natives. His calm and controlled fighting technique indicates the discipline, concentration, and dependability that define him as a person.

Furthermore, there is a unique sense of leadership and responsibility in Jikka’s personality. The safety of his fellow soldiers is of utmost importance to him, and he routinely prioritizes their needs over his own. Jikka’s moral integrity is another striking aspect of his character. Despite their mission’s difficult circumstances and dangerous nature, he remains unwavering in his convictions. His determination to do the right thing and stay true to his morals despite overwhelming odds adds a new dimension to the story’s exploration of morality.

1. Gabimaru the Hollow

Born in Iwagakure Village, Gabimaru became the most feared ninja due to his legendary battle prowess and unmatched level of invulnerability. His nickname “The Hollow” came about because he showed no emotion when fighting. 

Gabimaru may seem cold and heartless at first glance, yet he actually has soft spots, including a strong affection for his wife and a desire for peace. Throughout the show, this difference serves as Gabimaru’s motivation, inspiring him to persevere in the face of overwhelming obstacles and giving him a level of relatability that goes beyond his physical strength.
