Carl Paul Nassib, an outside linebacker with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League (NFL), was born on April 12, 1993. He played college football for the Penn State Nittany Lions, and as a senior in 2015, he was named a unanimous All-American. Nassib played for the Las Vegas Raiders before being selected by the Cleveland Browns in the third round of the 2016 NFL Draft. Nassib came out as gay in public and participated in an NFL game for the first time in 2021.

In West Chester, Pennsylvania, Nassib was born. He was a three-year letterwinner in football while he was a student at Malvern Preparatory School in Malvern. He was a defensive end and offensive tackle on the field. His junior and senior years saw the team finish with a 7-4 record with his assistance.

Nassib enrolled at Pennsylvania State University and was accepted as a walk-on to the Penn State football team in 2011.
