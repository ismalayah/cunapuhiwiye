Sakiko Tamagawa

Sakiko Tamagawa (Japanese: 玉川砂記子 Tamagawa Sakiko) is a Japanese voice actor for the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Tamagawa was born on January 20, 1962 in Tokyo, Japan. Tamagawa's only role on the series is that of recurring character Liza.
