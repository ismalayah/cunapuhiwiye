Jeremy Camp Kids With Wife Adrienne Camp And Family

July 15, 2024

Jeremy Camp has three kids with his wife Adrienne Camp and Melissa Lynn Henning-Camp. Jeremy Camp’s family consists of five members.

Jeremy is an American contemporary Christian musician, author, and songwriter, who is known for a mixture of ballads and up-tempo songs with a rock influence.

He released his independent debut studio album, Burden Me in 2000 and his follow-up album, Stay, under the label BEC Recordings in 2002.

As of now, Camp has released 11 albums, of which five have been gold-certified by the RIAA and two live albums.

He had a major breakthrough following the release of his album Beyond Measure, which charted at the No.1 spot six back-to-back times on AC Radio.

As for his accolades, the singer has won five GMA Dove Awards, and four ASCAP “Songwriter of the Year”  Awards, and also earned a nomination for the Grammy Award for Best Pop/Contemporary Gospel Album.

Jeremy Camp Family

Jeremy Camp family consisted of his mom and dad, and his three siblings. Camp was born to a religious household

He was born on January 12, 1978, in Lafayette, Indiana, U.S. After graduating from high school, Camp attended Calvary Chapel Bible College for two years and earned an Associate Degree in theology.

As a teenager, the singer lived a double life: Getting drunk and partying behind his parents’ backs while showing up intact on Sunday church sessions.

Jeremy Parents Were Very Religious

Jeremy Camp parents are Tom Camp and Teri Camp. Tom is his father while Teri is his mother.

Tom was a religious person. He served as a pastor at Harvest Chapel, which is associated with Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa.

Camp’s dad use to take him to the local prisons or boys’ homes to minister through words and music. Tom was the sole reason his son grew a close affinity to Christianity.

As a teenager his eyes were opened to the remarkable impact pastoring and ministering had on people’s lives. Prior to his role as a pastor, Tom was a worship leader.

He also taught the gospel singer how to play the guitar which sparked his passion for music and entertainment.

Teri and Tom made an appearance in an interview on the stage hosted by Sunrise Church Lafayette where they spoke about their son’s career and his upcoming documentary film.

The Gospel Singer Has 3 Siblings

Jeremy Camp siblings are Joshua Camp, Jared Camp, and April Camp. Joshia and Jared are his brothers while April is his sole sister.

Not much is known about Joshua and April, however, Jared was in the spotlight alongside his brother as they were in the same band.

As per a short film on YouTube, Jared used to play guitar for the band for many years until he eventually parted ways in 2012.

Jared is happily married to his wife Heather Camp and also has a son, Harvey.

The Singer Has Been Married Twice

Jeremy Camp wife is Adrienne Camp who is his second marriage. He was previously married to the late Melissa Lynn Henning-Camp.

The singer was first married to Melissa who sadly passed away months after their marriage. Following her passing, he married his current partner Adrienne.

Adrienne Camp

Adrienne Camp is a South African singer and songwriter. She is widely known as the lead vocalist of the Christian pop-rock band Benjamin Gate.

She crossed paths with the singer when he was touring in 2002 to promote his recently released album, Stay. The couple decided to take the next step and eventually married on December 15, 2003.

Roughly 17 years after their marriage, the two Christian musicians co-wrote and released their first EP in the midst of the pandemic in 2020.

The pair also released a book in 2020 ‘In Unison: The Unfinished Story of Jeremy and Adrienne Camp’, which focuses on their marriage, their faith, and obstacles during their 17 years together.

Adrienne has also been featured in many of her husband’s songs and music videos and also performs alongside him on tours.

Melissa Lynn Henning-Camp 

Jeremy first wife Melissa was an American homemaker. The two met each other in 1999 while worshiping at a small Bible study.

They instantly felt the chemistry and started dating shortly. After dating for a brief period, Melisa ended their relationship being fearful of God’s plan for her.

During their time apart, Melissa was diagnosed with ovarian cancer. Upon the discovery, the songwriter left everything behind to be there for his love.

The couple got engaged shortly after the diagnosis and planned their dream wedding. A month before the wedding, Melissa was miraculously free of cancer.

The couple happily married on October 21, 2000, and spent a memorable honeymoon in Hawaii. Unfortunately, cancer reappeared soon after and she passed away on February 5, 2001.

Heartbroken over the tragic and premature passing of his partner, Camp composed the song “I Still Believe” a few weeks after her death.

Jeremy Camp Children

Jeremy Camp children are Bella Camp, Arie Camp, and Egan Thomas Camp. All three are from his marriage to Adrienne.

Bella Rose Camp

Bella is 18 years old. She was born on September 25, 2004. 

Bella recently graduated high school and is currently a member of YWAM (Youth With A Mission) where she traveled to countries such as Norway and Cambodia.

On April 15, 2022, she debuted her music career by performing on tour alongside her dad. She is active on Instagram as @bella.rose.camp with over 11.9k followers.

Arie Camp

Arie is the second-born child of the celebrity couple. She was born on April 5, 2006, and recently turned 17 years.

Like her parents, she is an aspiring musician and singer. She has released multiple songs with her elder sister, which she posts on her YouTube channel.

Arie also actively posts on her Instagram account with the username @arie.camp in which she has accumulated over 11.7k followers.

Egan Thomas Camp

Egan is the latest addition to the family of five. He was born on August 18, 2011, and is currently 11 years old.
