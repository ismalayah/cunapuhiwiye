Brandon Smiley was a well-recognized American comedian and actor.

He was widely famed as the son of a popular American stand-up comedian and media personality, Rickey Smiley.

Brandon was born on 9 February 1990.

He was the son of his father, Rickey Smiley whereas his mother’s details are still unknown.

Brandon had nine siblings in total.

He has three siblings named D’Essence, Malik, and Aaryan and the remaining are his half-siblings.

Regarding education, the comedian completed his graduation from Shippensburg University in Pennsylvania.

Brandon Smiley cause of death

In January, media outlets reported that Brandon had succumbed to an accidental overdose.

However, as reported on People, his dad, Rickey Smiley said his son died of fentanyl and ethanol toxicity.

Additionally, Brandon battled an addiction to painkillers for years, according to his father.

The manner of death was ruled as accidental, according to the autopsy report from the Jefferson County Medical Examiner’s Office.
