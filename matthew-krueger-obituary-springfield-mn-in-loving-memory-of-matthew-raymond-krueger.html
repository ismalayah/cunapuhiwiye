Matthew Raymond Krueger Accident: In loving memory of Matthew Raymond Krueger.

Matthew Raymond Krueger “Mattie” was a beloved son, brother, grandchild, cousin, friend and a bright light in the everyones life.

Mattie’s favorite activities to enjoy was riding in the fire truck, going to the races, or calling windmills – “windows.” He loved snuggling with mom and dad and would often sneak into their bed at night, his obituary said.

Mattie leaves behind his parents, grandparents, aunts and uncles, cousins and step family.
