Rick Moranis Biography and Wiki

Rick Moranis is an accomplished Canadian Actor, Comedian, Filmmaker and Musician who was born and brought up in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He has appeared on many notable films and series such as Ghostbusters, Little Shop of Horrors and Spaceballs.

When his wife dies of Cancer, he left the film industry for decades and became a full-time single father for his two children. While he was away, he occasionally provided voice overwork for some animated films particularly ‘Disney’s Brother Bear’ in 2003.

It his during this time that he embraced creative entertainment industry and in 2003, he released his first comedy albums and performed at fan conventions. He was away from the live-action films for about 23 years after which he confirmed upon appearing in the sequel to ‘I Shrunk the Kids’ in early 2020.

Rick Moranis Wife

Moranis was happily married to the late Ann Belsky who was a costume designer in 1986. Their marriage was fruitful as they were blessed with two adorable daughters named Rachel and Mitchell.

In February 1991, Ann succumbed to breast cancer and passed away. Since then he became a single father and it forced him to take an 18-years long break from acting in order to raise properly his children.

Rick Moranis Kids

Rick is a proud father of two wonderful daughter named Rachel and Mitchell. He raised his daughters as a single father after his wife Ann Belsky died from cancer.

He left the public and went home raising his children alone and he is very secretive about his family. He is much protective of his daughters and detailed information about them is not available in Public.

Rick Moranis Ghostbusters

Rick appeared in the 1984 Ghostbusters film as Louis Tully. Ghostbusters is a supernatural comedy film that was written by Dan Aykroyd and Harold Ramis, produced and directed by Ivan Reitman.
